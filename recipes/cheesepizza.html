Cheese pizza recipe

Description:

This cheese pizza will remind you of traditional Italian culture. You will be transported to either the canals of venice, the Roman colosseum, or the beautiful coastlines.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps:

\
  1. Apply marinara sauce to pizza dough
  2. Put cheese on pizza dough
  3. Cook pizza

Congrats! You just made a pizza!
