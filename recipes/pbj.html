Peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe

This PBJ will make you feel like a kid again. Its complex flavor will surely make you want another.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  1. Put peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Put jelly on other slice of bread
  3. Close sandwich

      4. Congrats! You just made a peanut butter sandwich!
